How it works

Bridging the Gap Between AI & Agriculture

AgroSmart AI utilizes machine learning algorithms (Random Forest Classifier) trained on extensive agronomic parameters to guide crop planning choices.

By examining critical physical and chemical metrics—such as Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Potassium, soil pH, and local weather indicators—our models output the highest probability matching crops, eliminating guesswork.
